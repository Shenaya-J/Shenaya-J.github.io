About Us

The Puple Orchid Group, which was established in 2008, focuses on the administration of hotels and restaurants as well as the advancement of indigenous crafts. Its objective is to provide tourists a thorough understanding of Sri Lanka; hence, it currently has a range of branches dispersed around Sri Lanka's breathtaking landscapes. Because we believe that every tourist is different, Purple Orchid Hotel makes every effort to cater our services to your preferences.

Swimming Pool

You may enjoy the gorgeous outdoor pool at our hotel any time of the year. A large sun terrace with lounge chairs, umbrellas, and towels surrounds the heated pool. In the pool, you may unwind and rest while taking in the beautiful views of the metropolitan skyline. The pool has a jacuzzi for adults and a small section for kids. Our pool is the ideal spot to relax and have fun, whether you want to swim, splash, or soak.

Best Food

There are several eating alternatives available at our hotel to suit your preferences and mood. In our restaurant, you may have a sumptuous breakfast buffet that includes a variety of hot and cold meals, fresh fruits, pastries, juices, and coffee. You may select from our ala carte menu for lunch and supper, which offers regional and international cuisine made with premium, fresh ingredients. Any time of day or night, you may order room service and eat in the convenience and privacy of your own space. Visit our bar to enjoy cocktails, beers, wines, soft drinks, and hors d'oeuvres, among other beverages. No matter what you're craving, our hotel meals will fulfill it.
