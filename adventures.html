Experience Sheer Happiness with Us

~Adventure Expereinces~

Kandy City Tour

Kandy is the last great monarchy of Sri Lanka and a major attraction for tourists who want to get a glimpse at all the historical landmarks around the city.

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Known for housing the Relic of the Tooth of the Buddha, this renowned landmark in Kandy is also known as Sri Dalada Maligawa.

Scenic Tour to Knuckles Mountains

Here's a mountain range that is like a realization of a child's imagination. The shape formed by it resembles the ups and downs of the knuckles, hence the name. These mountains are a big part of the Knuckles National Heritage & Wilderness Area. Enjoy all the above adventure expereinces during your stay at Purple Orchid Kandy.

Snorkeling

Embark on a mesmerizing underwater journey with our captivating snorkeling expedition! Dive into the crystal-clear waters and discover a vibrant world teeming with marine life and colorful coral reefs.

Surfing

Dive into the exhilarating world of surfing during your stay at Purple Orchid Hikkaduwa! Discover the ultimate coastal adventure as you ride the waves, feel the salty breeze against your skin, and experience the sheer thrill of balancing on your board.

Stilt fishing

Stay aligned when you cast a line. Portraits of fishermen on stilts might sweep you off your feet.

Yala National Park Safari

Experience the thrill of the wild, a rather arid land with the highest density of leopards in the world! While the elusive cat is the star here, 215 species of birds and over 44 diverse mammals call this expansive space of wonder their home. Enjoy this experience during your stay at Purple Orchid Yala.

Guided Nature Walk

The experience of staying with us at Purple Orchid Yala is the most authentic way to live in the forest. We are fortunate to see sights like a pack of wild boars ambling around our pool or an elephant sipping water from it.

Explore Situlpawwa

In the midst of Yala National Park, at the Situlpawwa rock temple, coexistence of calm and wilderness achieves its pinnacle. It was constructed in the second century BC and has an illustrious 2,200-year history.

Purple Orchid Hotels

Where comfort and luxury embrace you at every turn. Our hotel is more than just a place to stay, it's a gateway to a world of information and connection, making your stay with us unforgettable. Thank you for choosing us, and we look forward to welcoming you soon.
