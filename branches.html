Purple Orchid Kandy

The main branch of our hotels, The Purple Orchid Kandy , offers magnificent rooms that are all exquisitely furnished, with panoramic views of
the Hanthana mountain range and meticulously tended gardens that are adorned with opulent flora all year long.
We are prepared to welcome you to this historical region.

Purple Orchid Hikkaduwa

While you indulge in exquisite bliss among the sun-kissed beaches of southern Sri Lanka, explore the nearby beaches and landscapes as well as
the vibrant nightlife. Purple Orchid Hikkaduwa, which provides the finest lodging for your beach excursion, is the best place
to enjoy this magnificent city. Discover your "place of joy" at Purple Orchid Hikkaduwa.

Purple Orchid Yala

We're thrilled to hear how excited you are to meet our wild neighbors. In fact, we have romantic meals on a deck directly by the lake,
under a starry sky, and it's breathtaking. You might even see an elephant drinking water from the pool as you sip your drink at the bar,
or a herd of wild boar strolling by the pool while you relax. We're pretty good buddies.
They come by. Discover your "place of joy" at Purple Orchid Yala.
